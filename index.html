Cannon Spirit - Privacy Policy

The game will ask for the following permissions:

  • In-App purchase
  • Identity
  • Contacts
  • Network access

We use these in order to:

  • Be able to show advertisement in the game
  • Be able to ask you to login to your google account so you can:
    • Share your scores
    • View other's scores
    • Find your friends so you can share the game with them
    • Find your friends so you can view their scores

We DO NOT:

  • Share any user sensible information
  • Store any user sensible information

Any changes to this privacy policy will be available at:

  • https://ros3cin.github.io/cannon-spirit-policy/

The effective date of this policy is:

  • 03/09/2017
